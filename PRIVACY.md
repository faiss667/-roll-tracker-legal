Roll Tracker Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2026-05-11
This Privacy Policy describes how Roll Tracker ("the app", "we", "us") collects, uses,
and protects your information.
1. Who we are
Roll Tracker is built and operated by Faycal Debbouze. Contact:
support.rolltracker@gmail.com.
2. What we collect
When you use Roll Tracker we may collect and store the following:
Account data: email address, encrypted password.
Profile data: display name, age, weight, body part injuries you record,
belt rank, stripe count, and your preferred default class type.
Training data: training sessions you log (date, duration, type, partners,
notes, recovery score), tournaments you record, events and reminders you
schedule, badges you earn.
Coach / gym data (only if you create or join a gym): the gym name and
pairing/check-in codes, your gym membership, the section you belong to,
attendance check-ins, promotions, drill assignments, appreciation notes.
Device data: Expo push notification token (only used to send you
notifications you have opted into).
We do not collect: precise location, contacts, photos outside what you
explicitly attach, advertising identifiers, or browsing history.
3. How we use it
To provide the core training-tracking features inside the app.
To let coaches you have joined run classes and send you appreciation,
promotions, and drills.
To deliver push notifications for events, reminders, and coach activity that
you have opted into.
We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers.
4. Where it lives
Account, training, and gym data are stored in our backend on
Supabase, hosted in the EU. Some non-sensitive
preferences (e.g. session draft, last-used filters) are also cached on your
device in standard app storage. Your password is never stored on your device
— authentication is handled by Supabase using industry-standard hashing.
5. Your rights
You can:
View and edit your data inside the app.
Sign out of your account at any time.
Request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing
support.rolltracker@gmail.com. We will action requests within 30 days.
If you are in the EU/UK you have additional rights under GDPR
(access, rectification, erasure, portability, objection). Email us to exercise
them.
6. Children
Roll Tracker is intended for users aged 13 and over. If your gym uses Roll Tracker to track
younger students, the gym (the data controller in that case) is responsible for
obtaining parental consent.
7. Security
We use industry-standard practices: TLS for all transport, hashed passwords,
row-level security on the database. No system is perfectly secure; if a breach
ever affects your data we will notify you.
8. Changes to this policy
We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect
changes. Material changes will be announced inside the app.
9. Contact
Questions: support.rolltracker@gmail

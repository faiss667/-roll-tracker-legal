Common questions
How do I join my gym?
Ask your coach to open Roll Tracker → Profile → Share gym. They will
either show you a QR code to scan or send you an invite message with
instructions. Open the app → Profile → Scan QR to join.
I'm not receiving push notifications
Make sure notifications are enabled for Roll Tracker in your phone's
Settings → Notifications.
Inside the app, go to Profile → Notification settings and check the
relevant toggles are on.
Push notifications are only delivered to installed builds (TestFlight or
App Store). They do not work inside the Expo Go preview app.
How do I reset my password?
On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot password. Enter your email and follow
the link we send you.
How do I export or delete my data?
Email debbouze.faycal24@gmail.com with the subject "Data export" or
"Account deletion". We will action the request within 30 days. See our
Privacy Policy
for full details on your rights.
A coach added me to the wrong gym / section
Ask the coach to remove you in their app (Students tab → your name →
Remove). If you can't reach them, email us and we will help.
The app crashed / data looks wrong
Email us with the details listed at the top of this page. If your training
data has gone missing, do not delete and reinstall the app first — contact
us so we can help recover it.
